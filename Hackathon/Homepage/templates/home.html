Physical Resources Sharing & Services

With KD community, PSA goes beyond just being a commercial landlord. Potentially, there are opportunities to offer different service offerings that could benefit the KD community. Services that PSA could offer to bring value to the KD Community would fall under the broad categories below

  • PSA as a broker on sharing of physical resources where operation synergies could be reaped in terms of warehouse space, handling equipment (forklift), trailers and pallets.
  • PSA as a master service provider to offer common services including supply of forklifts, supply of labour for container unstuffing/stuffing, container trucking, container storage, railing of cargoes, between warehouses, permit documentations.

A marketplace for the Cargo Consolidators/Freight Forwarders

PSA as a marketplace for the Cargo Consolidators/Freight Forwarders to sell cargo space in container for certain trade lane or destination that they are unable to fill with their own cargoes to freight forwarder who need to buy cargo space (co-load) as they do not have the base volume for that trade lane or destination.
